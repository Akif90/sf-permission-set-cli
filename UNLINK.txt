How to unlink permcraft CLI
===========================

To remove the global "permcraft" command from your system:

1. Navigate to the project directory:
   cd /Users/akif/Project/salesforce-permission-cli

2. Run:
   npm unlink permcraft

This removes the global symlink. The project files remain untouched.

To verify it was removed:
   permcraft --version
   (should show "command not found")
